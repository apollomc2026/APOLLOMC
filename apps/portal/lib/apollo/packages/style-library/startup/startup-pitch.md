# Startup Pitch — Style Specification
**Version:** 1
**Industry:** Startup

## Typography
- Heading font: Inter
- Body font: Inter
- Heading sizes: H1 36pt bold, H2 24pt semibold, H3 18pt medium, H4 14pt semibold
- Body size: 14pt (presentation-optimized, larger for slide readability)
- Body size for narrative documents: 11pt
- Line height: 1.6
- Stat callout size: 56pt Inter bold
- Caption and source: 10pt Inter light

## Color palette
- Primary: #3A86FF — use for headings, primary chart series, key metric numbers, and CTA elements
- Secondary: #8338EC — use for secondary data series, accent gradients, and category highlights
- Accent: #FF006E — use for urgency indicators, market opportunity callouts, and emphasis arrows
- Success: #06D6A0 — use for traction metrics, growth arrows, and positive trend indicators
- Body text: #1A1A2E
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Slide section backgrounds: alternate between #FFFFFF and #F0F4FF
- Card/callout background: #F7F9FF
- Chart palette: #3A86FF, #8338EC, #FF006E, #06D6A0, #FFB703

## Layout rules
- Page size: 16:9 widescreen (13.33in x 7.5in) for presentations; 8.5in x 11in for narrative documents
- Margins: 0.6in all sides for slides; 0.85in for documents
- Section spacing: one slide per major topic for presentations; 24pt before H1 for documents
- Content per slide: maximum 6 lines of text OR one key visual with supporting caption
- Column structure: flexible — single, two-column, or three-column based on content type
- Page/slide numbers: bottom right, 10pt, #AAAAAA
- No headers on slides — let content fill the space
- Footer on slides: company logo bottom-left, slide number bottom-right

## Formatting conventions
- One big number per slide for key metrics: 56pt stat + one-line context below
- Market size as a series of concentric circles or TAM/SAM/SOM funnel
- Traction slide: hockey stick chart or milestone timeline with growth arrows
- Team slide: headshot placeholders with name, title, and one-line credential
- Business model: simple flow diagram with revenue arrows in primary blue
- Competitive landscape: 2x2 matrix with company positioned in upper-right quadrant
- Use case / customer journey: 3-5 step horizontal flow with icons
- Ask slide: specific dollar amount, use of funds breakdown as horizontal stacked bar
- Bullet points: short phrases, not sentences — maximum 8 words per bullet
- Icons encouraged throughout for quick visual comprehension
- Gradient backgrounds permitted: #3A86FF to #8338EC for hero slides

## Tone and voice
- Compelling, visionary, and momentum-driven
- Write as a founder presenting to Tier 1 VCs
- Open with the problem — make it visceral and relatable in one sentence
- Every slide answers one question: "Why now?" "How big?" "Why us?" "What traction?"
- Numbers tell the story: "$4.2B market," "312% YoY growth," "47 enterprise customers"
- Confidence without arrogance: "We have built" not "We are disrupting" (show, do not claim)
- Future tense for roadmap: "By Q4 2027, we will" — specific and time-bound
- Social proof: name-drop customers, investors, advisors where applicable
- The ask is clear and specific: "$3M Series A to achieve [specific milestone]"
- Short sentences. Sentence fragments acceptable on slides. Let visuals carry the detail.

## What this style MUST produce
- Investor-grade pitch materials that follow the standard VC pitch flow
- One key message per slide — no information overload
- Large-format stat callouts for every key metric (56pt numbers)
- TAM/SAM/SOM market sizing visualization
- Traction chart showing growth trajectory
- Competitive positioning matrix
- Team slide with credentials
- Clear ask with use-of-funds breakdown
- Blue-to-purple visual identity consistent throughout
- Page/slide count: per deliverable type specification (typically 12-20 slides)
- All sections in the order specified by the deliverable module
