Executive Summary

Our understanding is that Reed Partners faces inconsistent portfolio-review documentation across deals and significant senior-partner time investment in final editing. We propose a three-phase workflow modernization engagement over 12 weeks to deliver a structured intake and generation system anchored to Reed Partners brand standards. This fixed-fee engagement will produce audited current-state findings, system design specifications, validated pilot deliverables, and complete rollout materials.

Understanding of Need

Reed Partners Capital is evaluating modernization of its portfolio-review documentation workflow. The current process produces inconsistent investor-facing materials across deals, creating brand dilution and operational inefficiency. Senior partners currently invest significant time in final-pass editing to achieve acceptable quality standards. This manual intervention creates bottlenecks in deal velocity and diverts partner attention from higher-value client engagement activities.

The challenge extends beyond document formatting to encompass workflow standardization, quality control, and scalable processes that maintain Reed Partners' professional standards while reducing senior-level editing requirements.

Proposed Approach

We propose a three-phase engagement structured to minimize disruption while delivering measurable workflow improvement. Phase 1 conducts a current-state audit across recent transactions to establish baseline metrics and identify specific inefficiencies. This audit will document existing templates, approval workflows, time allocation by role, and quality variance patterns.

Phase 2 designs a structured intake and generation system anchored to Reed Partners brand standards. This phase produces technical specifications, workflow diagrams, and template frameworks that address identified inefficiencies while maintaining deal-team autonomy. Design validation occurs through partner review gates before implementation planning begins.

Phase 3 executes phased rollout with partner-level validation. We will produce three pilot deal memos using the new system, collect partner feedback, refine processes, and deliver complete training materials. The rollout plan includes success metrics, adoption timelines, and ongoing maintenance protocols.

Each phase includes defined validation checkpoints with Reed Partners leadership to ensure alignment with firm standards and operational requirements.

Deliverables

1. Current-state audit memorandum documenting existing workflow inefficiencies, time allocation patterns, and quality variance analysis

2. Template system design specification including technical architecture, workflow integration points, and brand compliance protocols

3. Partner-validated pilot deliverables consisting of three deal memos produced through the new system with documented time savings and quality metrics

4. Rollout plan and training materials including implementation timeline, success metrics, partner training protocols, and ongoing maintenance procedures
