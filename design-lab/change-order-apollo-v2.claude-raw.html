CHANGE ORDER

Reference to Original SOW

This Change Order amends SOW-2026-0042 dated 1 May 2026 between Apollo MC and Summit Property Management.

Change Description

Summit has acquired two additional properties during the engagement period and requested inclusion of those sites under the existing deployment.

Effective 15 June 2026, the Scope section of the original SOW is amended to include the following additions:

  • Add hardware deployment at 2 additional Summit-acquired sites
  • Extend technician training to new maintenance staff at acquired sites
  • Extend 60-day support window to cover new deployments

Schedule Impact

Add 4 weeks to the original timeline. Revised end date: 28 September 2026.

Fee Impact

Additional fee of $18,000. Revised total engagement fee: $105,500.

Acceptance

This Change Order becomes effective 15 June 2026 upon acceptance by both parties. All other terms and conditions of the original SOW dated 1 May 2026 remain in full force and effect except as modified herein.
