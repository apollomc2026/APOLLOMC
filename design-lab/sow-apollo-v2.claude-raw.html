SCOPE OF WORK

Client: Summit Property Management
Project: Atlas Access Control Deployment
Period: May 1, 2026 through August 31, 2026

Executive Summary

This engagement involves the deployment of the Atlas field intelligence platform across Summit Property Management's 12 properties. The project includes complete hardware installation, comprehensive technician training for Summit maintenance staff, and 60 days of post-deployment support. The engagement is structured as a fixed-fee project with clearly defined deliverables and acceptance criteria.

Scope of Work

  • Hardware deployment: Installation and configuration of Atlas access control systems at all 12 Summit properties, including initial system testing and connectivity verification.
  • Staff training: Development and delivery of technician training program tailored to Summit's maintenance staff, covering system operation, basic troubleshooting, and routine maintenance procedures.
  • Post-deployment support: Technical support and issue resolution during the 60-day stabilization period following deployment completion.
  • Documentation and reporting: Comprehensive documentation of deployment activities, system configurations, and final project summary.

Deliverables

  1. Hardware deployment at 12 sites: Complete installation of Atlas access control hardware with functional testing at each location. Acceptance requires successful operation of all installed components for 48 hours without critical failures.
  2. Technician training program for Summit maintenance staff: Structured training curriculum delivered to designated Summit personnel, including training materials and certification documentation. Acceptance requires completion of training by all designated staff members.
  3. 60-day post-deployment support: Technical support services including remote diagnostics, issue resolution, and system optimization during the support period. Acceptance is automatic upon completion of the 60-day period.
  4. Final deployment report: Comprehensive project summary including site-specific configurations, training records, and system performance baselines. Acceptance requires written approval of report content and format by Summit's designated project owner.

Timeline

Phase 1 - Site Assessment and Planning: May 1-15, 2026
Phase 2 - Hardware Deployment: May 16 - July 15, 2026
Phase 3 - Training Delivery: July 16-31, 2026
Phase 4 - Support Period: August 1-31, 2026 (60 days from deployment completion)
Final Report Delivery: September 15, 2026

Assumptions and Exclusions

Assumptions:

  • Summit will provide necessary facility access during standard business hours
  • Existing network infrastructure at each property is adequate for Atlas system requirements
  • Summit will designate a primary point of contact for coordination and decision-making
  • Required permits and approvals for hardware installation will be obtained by Summit

Exclusions:

  • Network infrastructure upgrades or modifications
  • Ongoing maintenance beyond the 60-day support period
  • Additional training sessions beyond the initial program
  • Hardware replacement due to damage or normal wear

Fees and Payment Terms

The total project fee is $87,500, payable as follows:

  • 25% upon execution of this agreement
  • 50% upon completion of hardware deployment (Phase 2)
  • 25% upon delivery of final deployment report

Payment terms are net 30 days from invoice date. Late payments may incur a service charge of 1.5% per month.

Acceptance Criteria

Each deliverable is accepted upon written confirmation from Summit's designated project owner that the deliverable meets the specifications outlined in this scope of work.
