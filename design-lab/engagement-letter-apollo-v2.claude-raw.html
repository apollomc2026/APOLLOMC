CLIENT ENGAGEMENT LETTER

Dear Reed Partners Capital:

We are pleased to confirm our engagement to provide advisory services to Reed Partners Capital, subject to the terms and conditions set forth in this letter. This engagement letter confirms our mutual understanding of the services to be performed, the basis upon which fees will be charged, and other terms of our professional relationship.

Engagement Scope

We will provide strategic advisory services on digital transformation initiatives, with focus on document workflow automation and vendor evaluation. Our services will commence on May 15, 2026, and will include analysis of current systems, identification of optimization opportunities, vendor assessment and due diligence, implementation planning, and ongoing strategic guidance as requested.

The scope of our engagement is specifically limited to advisory services. We will not provide implementation services, technology installation, or ongoing operational support unless separately agreed upon in writing.

Fees and Billing

Our fee arrangement consists of a monthly retainer of $12,000, billed in advance on the 1st of each month. The retainer covers our advisory services as described above and includes reasonable access for consultation and strategic guidance. Any services requested outside the defined scope will be subject to separate agreement and billing.

Payment terms are net 30 days from invoice date. Late payments may be subject to interest charges at the rate of 1.5% per month.

Responsibilities of Each Party

Reed Partners Capital will provide timely access to relevant personnel, systems documentation, current vendor agreements, and other materials necessary for our advisory work. Sarah Chen, COO, will serve as the primary point of contact and will coordinate access to other team members as needed.

We will provide strategic advisory services as outlined above with Jon Sargent, Managing Partner, serving as the primary point of contact. We will deliver analysis, recommendations, and guidance in a timely manner and will maintain regular communication regarding project status and findings.

Confidentiality

We acknowledge that during the course of this engagement, we may have access to confidential and proprietary information of Reed Partners Capital. We agree to maintain the confidentiality of all such information and to use it solely for the purpose of performing the services described in this letter. This confidentiality obligation will survive the termination of this engagement.

Termination

Either party may terminate this engagement at any time upon thirty (30) days written notice to the other party. Upon termination, we will be entitled to payment for all services performed through the date of termination. Any retainer amounts paid in advance for periods after termination will be refunded on a pro-rata basis.

If the foregoing terms accurately reflect our mutual understanding, please sign and return one copy of this letter to indicate your acceptance. We look forward to working with Reed Partners Capital on this engagement.

Very truly yours,
