Reed Partners Capital
Attention: Sarah Chen, COO

Dear Ms. Chen,

We are pleased to confirm our engagement to provide advisory services to Reed Partners Capital, subject to the terms and conditions set forth in this letter. This engagement letter memorializes our prior discussions and establishes the framework for our professional relationship.

Engagement Scope

We will provide strategic advisory services on digital transformation initiatives, with focus on document workflow automation and vendor evaluation. Our services will include analysis of current operational workflows, identification of automation opportunities, evaluation of technology vendors and solutions, and strategic recommendations for implementation planning.

This engagement is advisory in nature. We will provide recommendations and strategic guidance, but implementation decisions and vendor selection remain solely within your discretion. Our role does not include project management of implementation activities unless separately agreed in writing.

Fees and Billing

Our fee arrangement is a monthly retainer of $12,000, billed in advance on the 1st of each month. The retainer covers our advisory services as described above and includes reasonable availability for consultation and strategic guidance.

Payment terms are net 15 days from invoice date. Late payments may incur a service charge of 1.5% per month. Any expenses incurred on your behalf will be billed separately at cost.

Responsibilities of Each Party

Reed Partners Capital will provide timely access to relevant personnel, documentation, and systems necessary for our advisory work. You will designate Sarah Chen, COO, as the primary point of contact for this engagement and ensure appropriate stakeholder availability for meetings and information gathering sessions.

Apollo MC will provide strategic advisory services through Jon Sargent, Managing Partner, who will serve as your primary point of contact. We will deliver our recommendations and analysis in a professional and timely manner, drawing upon our expertise in digital transformation and workflow optimization.

Confidentiality

We acknowledge that we may have access to confidential and proprietary information belonging to Reed Partners Capital. We agree to maintain the confidentiality of such information and not to disclose it to third parties without your prior written consent. This obligation survives the termination of our engagement.

Similarly, you agree to maintain the confidentiality of our methodologies, recommendations, and work product developed specifically for this engagement.

Termination

Either party may terminate this engagement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, you will pay all fees and expenses incurred through the termination date. Our obligations regarding confidentiality will survive termination.

We look forward to working with Reed Partners Capital on this engagement. Please indicate your acceptance of these terms by signing and returning one copy of this letter.
